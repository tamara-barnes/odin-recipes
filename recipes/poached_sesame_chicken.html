Poached Sesame Chicken

What is Poached Sesame Chicken?

Poached Chicken with asian sesame sauce!

This has been one of my staples for an easy, quick and nutritious meal. The sauce is delicious - its super creamy with a hint of sweetness and tang.

Packed full of protein and healthy fats, this meal will leave you satisfied!

Save for later and give it a go!

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 1 x Chicken Breast, about 250g
  • 4-6 thinly sliced ginger peices
  • 1 x Spring Onion, cut into quarters

For the Sauce

  • 3 x Tbsp unhulled tahini
  • 4 x Tsp minced ginger
  • 1 x Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 x Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 Tsp rice wine vinegar
  • 2 Tsp Rayu (Japanese chilli oil)
  • 2 Tbsp thinly sliced spring onion

To Serve

  • 1 x cucumber, thinly sliced into julienne strips

Steps

  1. Fill your pot with water so your chicken will be submerged with at least 5cm of water. Add the sliced ginger, spring onions and bring to boil.
  2. Meanwhile, use a fork to punch a few holes through the thicker end of the chicken breast on both sides. This will help the chicken cook evenly.
  3. Add the Chicken, then immediately reduce to a low simmer and cover with a lid. Continue cooking for 5 minutes then turn off the heat.
    Leave to rest for 30-45 minutes. To check doneness, the internal temp of the thicker part should read about 65 degrees, and juices should run clear when pierced.
  4. Transfer the chicken to a heat-proof container. Once its slightly cooled, pull the chicken apart into strips. Cover and place in the fridge to cool.
  5. Add all sauce ingredients into a mixing bowl except for the spring onions. Stir until combined. If its too thick, add 1tbsp of water at a time to thin out.
    Add in your spring onions and stir gently.
  6. Place the cucumber and chicken on a serving plate. Pour the sauce on top of the chicken. Enjoy!
