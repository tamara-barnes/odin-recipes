Poached Sesame Chicken

What is Poached Sesame Chicken?

Poached Chicken with asian sesame sauce!

This has been one of my staples for an easy, quick and nutritious meal. The sauce is delicious - its super creamy with a hint of sweetness and tang.

Packed full of protein and healthy fats, this meal will leave you satisfied!

Save for later and give it a go!

Ingredients

For the Chicken

  • 1 x Chicken Breast, about 250g
  • 4-6 thinly sliced ginger peices
  • 1 x Spring Onion, cut into quarters

For the Sauce

  • 3 x Tbsp unhulled tahini
  • 4 x Tsp minced ginger
  • 1 x Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 x Tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 Tsp rice wine vinegar
  • 2 Tsp Rayu (Japanese chilli oil)
  • 2 Tbsp thinly sliced spring onion
